# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or
invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing empathy
and kindness, respecting differing opinions and experiences, giving and gracefully
accepting constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments,
publishing others' private information without permission, and other conduct which could
reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to
the maintainers at **markauto75@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and
security of the reporter of any incident.
